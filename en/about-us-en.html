About SJJ

SJJ Railway Materials Supply (China) Limited is a Hong Kong-based engineering supply and service company specializing in railway materials, engineering products, project equipment, and technical support for infrastructure and transportation-related projects. Established in 2000, the company has built a strong service foundation in the railway, public transport, airport, building equipment, and other engineering sectors in Hong Kong and overseas markets. The company's services encompass material supply, product procurement, import and export, technical consulting, design-related coordination, and project-based engineering support. Leveraging its practical experience in the railway and engineering environment, SJJ supports contractors, engineering companies, and project teams in meeting demanding project requirements, including product reliability, site suitability, compliance, delivery coordination, and long-term service support. SJJ's work is closely related to major infrastructure and transportation projects in Hong Kong, serving engineering partners involved in railway operations, bus operations, airport facilities, electromechanical systems, and large building equipment projects. In addition to its supply and service operations in Hong Kong, SJJ also obtains manufacturing and quality assurance capabilities through its mainland China subsidiaries. These capabilities help the Group coordinate product development, production, quality control, and technical support for railway and engineering applications. The Mainland subsidiaries possess industry qualifications such as ISO 9001:2015 and EN15085, supporting the Group's commitment to quality, reliability, and professional standards. Leveraging over two decades of industry experience, SJJ combines technical knowledge, procurement capabilities, project understanding, and customer-centric service to provide practical and reliable solutions for engineering applications. As Hong Kong continues to invest in infrastructure, transportation systems, and engineering maintenance, SJJ will continue to focus on supporting customers by providing reliable materials, suitable engineering products, and responsive project services.
